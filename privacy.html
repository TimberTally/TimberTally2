How your data is collected, stored, and protected

Short version: TimberTally stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information with anyone. No account is required. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

3. Data We Collect and Why

TimberTally collects only the data you actively enter or choose to enable. All data is stored locally on your device using the browser's localStorage and service worker cache. No data is transmitted to any server.

  • Timber Inventory Data — Plot numbers, species, DBH, log count, cut/UGS status, and optional per-tree notes that you type in. Stored locally to support data entry, calculations, and CSV export.
  • Stand Notes — Free-form notes you enter on the Notes tab. Stored locally and exported as a .txt file only when you initiate an export.
  • Forestry Settings — Your selected BAF, Log Rule, Form Class, graph preference, and dark mode setting. Stored locally so your preferences persist between sessions.
  • Custom Species List — Any species names you add or remove. Stored locally on your device.
  • Saved Projects — Named datasets you choose to save using the Projects feature. Stored locally on your device.
  • Session Recovery Data — A temporary snapshot of unsaved entries, stored locally to allow recovery if the app closes unexpectedly. Cleared once you confirm or dismiss the recovery prompt.
  • GPS / Location Data (optional) — Latitude and longitude captured only when you tap the "GPS" button and grant permission via the standard browser/OS prompt. Location data is stored locally with the corresponding tree entry and is never transmitted. You can use the app fully without enabling location access.
  • Device Orientation (optional) — Used only when you open the compass feature. Accessed in real time to display a heading and not stored in any form.
  • App Cache Files — The service worker caches the app's own HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and icon files on your device for offline use. These are application files, not personal data.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

TimberTally does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information (no login required)
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking cookies
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports sent to any external service
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any data from contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or other device sensors beyond the optional GPS and compass features described above

5. How Data Is Stored and Protected

All user-entered data is stored exclusively in your device's browser localStorage and is never uploaded, synchronized, or backed up to any external server or cloud service. The security of your locally stored data depends on the security of your own device and browser. We do not implement additional encryption beyond what your browser natively provides.

Clearing your browser's site data or uninstalling the app will permanently erase all locally stored information, including saved projects, custom species, settings, and any unexported inventory data.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, license, or disclose your data to any third party — ever. The only way data leaves the app is when you initiate a "Save CSV" export, which saves files directly to your device. What you do with those exported files is entirely your choice.

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. The only external resource the app loads is the Chart.js library from a public CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net) for rendering graphs. This CDN request does not include any user data.

8. Children's Privacy

TimberTally is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app stores no data on any server, there is no mechanism by which children's data could be transmitted to us. If you have concerns, please contact us at timbertallyapp@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights and Data Controls

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us, you have complete control:

  • Access — View all your data directly in the app at any time.
  • Export — Use "Save CSV" to export your data in a portable format.
  • Delete entries — Select entries and tap Delete, or use "Delete All Data" to clear everything.
  • Delete projects/species/settings — Managed directly from the Projects and Species tabs.
  • Full wipe — Clearing your browser's site data for TimberTally, or uninstalling the app, removes all locally stored information permanently.

Because we never receive your data, we are unable to fulfill data access or deletion requests on our end — there is simply nothing for us to hold or delete.

10. Data Retention

Data persists on your device until you delete it yourself or clear browser site data. We hold no copies of your data and have no retention schedule to disclose.

11. Security

We have designed TimberTally to minimize privacy risk by keeping all data local. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage that could be breached to expose your information. The security of your locally stored data is governed by your device's own security model.
